Plastic Waste

The Problem

Plastic pollutes at every stage (extraction, production, consumption, and disposal). The overload of plastic waste in our system is devastating our environment.

What Others are Doing

  1. Canada aims to ban single-use plastics by 2021.
  2. Peru has restricted single use plastics already.
  3. San Diego, California, has banned styrofoam food and drink containers.
  4. DC has banned plastic straws.
  5. 250 groups have launched a massive global plastic partership!

5 Things You Can Do

  1. Stop Using Plastic Bags: Bring a reusable bag when you go shopping.
  2. Use a Reusable Waterbottle
  3. Shop at Local Farmers' Markets: Opt for unpackaged produce.
  4. Responsibly Dispose Your Trash: Don't litter and know your recyclables.
  5. Support Plastic Bans: Call your local representative and voice your ideas!
